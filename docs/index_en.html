My Methods

DESIGN THINKING

"Fail often so you can succeed sooner"

- Tom Kelley

Design Thinking is an approach to solving problems and developing new ideas. The goal is to find solutions that are convincing from the user's point of view. In contrast to other innovation methods, Design Thinking can or is sometimes not described as a method or process, but rather as an approach based on the three equally important basic principles of team, space and process.

Quelle: https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Design_Thinking

EXPERIENCE DESIGN

"It's not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

- Steve Jobs

User Experience (UX), User Interface (UI), Service Design, Customer Experience (CX) are just a few of the terms you will encounter when dealing with design. In the end, it's always about the experience of the people with whom I always start my work. That's why I call it Experience Design

AGILE WORK

"The goal of New Work is not to free people from work, but to transform work so that it produces free, self-determined human beings"

- Frithjof Bergmann

Digital transformation requires a change in the way we work. Becoming more agile and innovative affects the entire organization and not just individual employees. With a lot of experience in agile working and the broad view of the systemic organizational developer, I help you to pave your own way.
